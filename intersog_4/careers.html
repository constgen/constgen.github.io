Careers

INTERSOG believes that our success is directly related to the quality of our employees. We are always looking for bright and talented individuals to join our team, particularly those interested in working in a fast-paced, high-growth, high-technology environment. Our corporate culture attracts motivated individuals who thrive in a collaborative setting, where hard work and having fun are always in balance.

We offer our employees:

  • Challenging and Engaging Projects
  • Career and Skill Development Opportunities
  • A Casual Work Environment
  • Training Opportunities
  • Competitive Compensation and Bonus Opportunities.
  • Your specialized skills, motivation and desire to grow as an IT professional will ensure your success with INTERSOG.

Please familiarize yourself with our current career opportunities. If no current openings fit your skill set, please feel free to forward your resume to: jobs@intersog.com.
