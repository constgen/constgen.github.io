Morton Salt Pro


About the company

Morton Salt, Inc., a Chicago-based company continuing a Morton Salt business dating back to 1848, is North America's authority on salt and a leading producer of salt for grocery, water softening, ice control, agricultural, and industrial uses. Morton introduced the nation to the Morton Umbrella Girl and the now-famous slogan, "When It Rains It Pours" in 1914. Since that time, Morton Salt products have graced the shelves of more homes throughout the country than any other brand of salt.

Challenge

Morton Salt, Inc. approached digi117 to create a mobile application to help snow professionals manage selection and usage of Morton’s complete line of professional ice melting products.  The primary goal of the application was to utilize scientific research to identify the optimal Morton ice melter and application rate for specific environmental conditions and surface area.  The app would essentially help snow professionals save time by (by recommending products that work faster in given conditions) and save money (by reducing melter over-application).

Result

Based on our client’s requests, digi117’s team of professional developers created the Morton Salt Pro application that takes input on external temperature conditions and surface area to identify the most suitable ice melting product and application rate for the snow professionals’ job. The application allows users to store and track site information and also provides application guidance for “non-optimal” melters in the event the snow professional does not have the app recommended melter available.  The application makes it easier for snow professionals to plan for snow events, and helps ensure consumer safety by highlighting application rates and products that most effectively keep sidewalks, driveways and steps clear of ice and snow. 

App Features:

- Customizable work site catalog
- Site detail histories including location and area
- Calculator to identify ice melter & quantity required
- Air/ surface temperature logging
- Work site surface area logging
- Product recommendation capability
- Morton Salt ice melting product library

Morton Salt Pro is compatible with iPhone, iPad, iPod Touch and Android devices. It’s a free application available for download on the Apple App Store and Android Market. There is also a desktop calculator tool available at www.mortonmelters.com for stationary use.

Forest Preserve District of Cook County


About the company

The Forest Preserve District of Cook County is the United States’ largest and oldest forest preserve system. Established in 1914, the District’s attractions, programming and services are very diverse and engaging. With Brookfield Zoo, Chicago Botanic Garden, 10 golf courses, four driving ranges, three aquatic centers, 10 sledding hills, five snowmobiling areas, 263 picnic groves, forty lakes and ponds, and 300 miles of paved and unpaved trails for visitors to enjoy, the District is the premier provider of safe, enjoyable, and affordable family fun in northeastern Illinois.

Challenge

Digi117 was approached on behalf of the client in order to utilize our industry leading mobile development team and unique creative strategies to build a versatile mobile application for the Forest Preserve District of Cook County. This application would be used to provide users with real-time access to a wide range of location-based services while enjoying The Forest Preserve District of Cook County’s many attractions and public areas.

Result

Digi117’s team of professional developers guided this project from its initial conceptual stage to its final execution. The end result of this work was the creation of The Forest Preserve District of Cook County’s application, in both iOS and BlackBerry compatible variations. The application’s central functionalities include providing users with information about Cook County Forest Preserve District’s many facilities, assets, and events. For user convenience, accessing the application’s vast array of contents is done by navigating and reviewing dynamic, location-based maps, and categorized informational sections.

Though a general search function is included, information can be found via several other inquiry methods as well. These include conducting searches by region, activity, or event, or by selecting any one of the many push-pin icons present on the application’s interactive maps. Data for each location is presented on a separate screen and includes a short description, as well as contact information for the given event or location, driving directions, and photos. Activity and Region search functions allow users to locate specified areas of interest, such as “Hiking”, “Golfing”, “Fishing”, “Nature center”, etc.

Forest Preserve District of Cook County application is compatible with iPhone, iPad, iPod Touch and Blackberry devices. It is available for download on the Apple App Store and BlackBerry App World.

About the company

Challenge

As a digital publishing group, ComboApp aims to differentiate itself from conventional publishing companies both in its operations as well as its approach to publishing as a whole. Starting with authored content, we set goal for ourselves: To create complete Learning Systems in the form of mobile applications had the capability to succeed at delivering practical educational solutions across every major mobile platform. These applications were created to assist people in studying professional fields ranging from business to finance. In order to carry out this ambitious and immensely challenging project - “World Academy Online” - ComboApp employed digi117’s professional team. Digi117’s task was to execute a wide range of full-cycle mobile strategies and solutions to connect customers with their education in new and innovative ways.

Result

Digi117’s dedicated efforts helped develop the World Academy Online brand from two independent educational systems created specifically for the mobile marketplace – ComboBook™ and Learning To-Go™.

ComboBook™ represents a revolutionary new system for self-education. This system creates a user-directed, interactive educational system from any content without requiring preexisting flashcards, tests or other study aides. It functions by converting stagnant text into dynamic and interactive education. The basic principle is as follows: students read a section of the text and before proceeding to the next section, they are asked a series of questions that could include various types of tests. These tests include: putting words in the right order, filling in the blanks within a short paragraph, finishing a statement with the proper variation or true/false questions. This learning system utilizes innovative new methods to analyze any authored content and use it as a base to generate spontaneous study tools, ultimately transforming content into and engaging and interactive experience that is suitable for self-educational purposes.

ComboBook Applications Feature:

  • Outline functionality.
  • In-app content search.
  • Page flip animation.
  • Adaptive test generation capability.
  • Comprehensive text analysis capability.
  • Text formatting functionality.

Learning To-Go™ is a single-source learning solution. This is a mobile educational system designed to encourage and enable independent study of original, authored material. Learning To-Go titles are both informative and affordable, providing users with a well-integrated, flexible learning experience which they can utilize on the go from anywhere and at any time. There are three primary categories in these innovative learning systems:

  • Text- In the form of articles with graphs, tables, formulas, pictures etc.
  • Flash Cards - Available for every chapter to help learn and test the material
  • Tests - For every chapter there is a set of questions to test students' retention of material

Combining text, flashcards, and self-tests, each Learning To-Go title creates a completely user-controlled educational experience. The system tracks students’ retention of material and reports back to them with a visual progress chart. Upon completion users will also have a solid understanding of the many important market influences and economic conditions that affect modern business and finance environments. More importantly, they will have gained real-world knowledge to identify and implement the strategies they have learned in order to achieve success in their own careers.

Learning To-Go Applications Feature:

  • Full course text divided by chapter.
  • Interactive flashcards for subject review.
  • Comprehensive chapter level tests.
  • Explanation of right and wrong answers.
  • Comprehensive chapter level tests.
  • A bookmark.
  • A glossary.
  • Offline access to material, no internet connection required.

The applications included into these learning systems are compatible with iPhone, iPad and iPod Touch and are available for download on the Apple App Store.

Challenge

The Enneagram Personality Full Test (known as the RHETI) has been independently scientifically validated to accurately identify the unique psychological makeup of an individual’s personality. Over 300,000 Enneagram Personality Full Tests have already been sold around the world in Chinese, Dutch, English, French, German, Korean, Polish, Russian, Spanish, and Swedish. INTERSOG aimed to develop a mobile application that would allow persons interested to take that immensely popular test by means of their mobile devices. In order to make this project a reality INTERSOG turned to its subsidiary digi117, a company most skilled in providing full-cycle strategic marketing and development solutions for creating unique mobile applications and breaking brands, products, and businesses through into the mobile limelight.

Result

Digi117 staff’s efforts culminated in development of the Enneagram Personality Full Test. This application recognizes 9 distinct personality types, the mix of which varies in each individual. In most cases, the Enneagram Personality Full Test reveals the user’s dominant personality type. Furthermore it indicates the relative strengths of the other eight personality types present in their psychological makeup. Results include personality type descriptions, structural patterns, as well as relationship and growth suggestions for each type.

Easily completed in about 40 minutes, the Enneagram Personality Full Test is a convenient tool for individuals as well as businesses and professionals, helping them to increase their insight into themselves or their clients. People around the world have found it exceptionally insightful and useful. Moreover, it is an especially valuable tool for HR professionals, since it allows the user to identify personality traits in existing employees in order to work with them more effectively.

The Enneagram Personality Full Test is compatible with iPhone, iPad, iPod Touch and Android devices. It is available for download on both the Apple App Store as well as the Android Market.
