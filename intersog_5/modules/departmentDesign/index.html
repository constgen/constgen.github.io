Our graphic and game design team will help you through every step of the game production process. Whether it’s working together to think of a great game concept to build, designing and developing your game into fruition, or promoting and launching the finished product into the mobile marketplace, we’re here. There are three main stages that make up a game’s production life cycle: Development, Design, and Promotion. Lets take a more detailed look at we can do for you at each of them:

Development Services:

UX Design

Our UX experts develop intelligent, coherent user experience designs. These designs give us a solid representation of the game in progress, a convenient intuitive scheme of users’ use flow and the game’s navigation, as well as a manual for the game itself. Our wealth of UX creation experience guarantees that we’ll design user experiences that vibe and connect with your end users.

Mockup Creation

Our Game Design Team creates interactive and detailed mock-ups of all screens of the game. This work is performed to provide the clear and precise vision and understanding of the future game’s functionality.

Concept Illustration

Our specialists are developing and drawing the concept of the future game in a highly creative and innovative way to ensure that we create a special, unique and popular application.

Character Design

Our team are tireless and crazy inventors and artists, with the imagination that has no limits.

It is our zealous passion to create charismatic and modern characters, which will capture the imagination of the users and gain their persistent sympathy and love.

You can find the samples of our previous works at our portfolio

“MockUp” Screen Creation

On the development stage of games production we also create “mock-up” screens which are much simpler and less pretentious than actual ones. “Mock-Up” screens help to visualize the game and provide the initial idea and understanding of how it will look in the future. “Mock-Up” screens also can be useful for presenting the future application to client’s investor, partners, etc.

Promotional Services:

Promotional Banners

The main function of promo banners is to capture the user’s attention and lead him to the game itself, available on App Store and/or Google Play. We are experts in creating eye-catching and spellbinding promo banners which are very effective in forming new flows of people interested in the downloading the game.

Promotional Game Videos

Our video artists are the masters of creating breathtaking and shining launch trailers for the games. The video presents the game at its best, gives the clear idea of ​​its essence and attracts users to try to play it.
