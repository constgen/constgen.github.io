iPhone & Android developers conference

International company INTERSOG invites Objective C developers, as well as anyone who has certain interest and experience in developing iPhone and Android apps to attend a conference that will take place on March 19, 2011 in Nikolaev, Ukraine.

This event will provide a unique opportunity to learn from author presentations, addressing various aspects of mobile applications/games development. You will hear presentations dedicated to programming, testing, usability and other aspects of our daily work.

This conference will focus not only on developers but on analysts, beta testers, managers, as well as everyone interested and involved in mobile apps/games marketing.

Specifically for this conference we are inviting the best specialists, who will gladly share their experience with you, including our American and Canadian colleagues.

Aside from presentations you will be able to share your experience during coffee breaks in a friendly, informal environment.

  1. Mobile User Interface
  2. NFC technology
  3. Marketing and Promotion iPhone application.
  4. Utilizing certain Google services, such as Google geocoding, Google places, Google Latitude, in iPhone/iPad apps development.
  5. Hidden rocks in porting iPhone apps to Bada 2.0
  6. Game engine - Shiva 3d
  7. Mobile WEB.

This conference will benefit anyone who considers their own professional growth as an important factor and cares about an industry progress as a whole.

Come and bring anyone interested in iPhone and Android app development.

Date and time - March 19, 2011 at 11:00am
Place - Nikolaev, Ukraine. Venu to be determined
The conference program will be available in the near future.
Admission - Free.

Raise The Village gets great media coverage!

As it's well known that any project and, especially a non-profit one, always strives for media attention to make people aware of its existence, to spread the word as much as possible and eventually to reach the right people who will help to move a project forward and let it prosper.

We are glad to report that RaiseTheVillage project has been mentioned by AOL editor Joe Osborne - http://blog.games.com/2011/01/06/games-coms-predictions-2011/ It's really delightful to read good words that reward all efforts have been put into the project and see how good your intentions resonate with people who spread the news about your project on such big and reputable media resource as AOL is. And so we really want to thank the AOL's editorial staff for giving the project such a great exposure and support!

But that's not it - the project has been covered in many other media outlets and if you want to check them all feel free to visit - http://www.raisethevillage.com/in-the-media.aspx To know more about the project visit http://www.raisethevillage.com To download the iPhone app, which works perfectly well on both iPod Touch and iPad as well visit - http://itunes.apple.com/us/app/raise-the-village/id398463247?mt=8
